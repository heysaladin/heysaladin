Hey, I’m Saladin

A Passionate Digital Product Designer.

I design digital products by understanding the problem and finding the right solution using multidisciplinary knowledge such as research, visual design, good interaction, and also supported by a little coding & business knowledge.

Solving Problem
Creatively

User Experience

Conduct research to understand a problem, define what can be done & solutions to solve the problem.

User Interface

The form of a prototype of a product to be able to solve a problem while still being easy to use by the user.

User Interaction

Creating a user interaction in the form of a prototype of a product to be able to solve a problem.

My Passionate
Creative Work Flow

In working on a case, I usually use the Human Centered Design approach by understanding what happened, finding the real problem, trying to solve the problem with creative ideas, making a prototype of the idea and of course testing the idea to the user whether the idea is true. what I submitted was able to solve the problem.

Discover, Define, Ideate, Prototype, Validate
